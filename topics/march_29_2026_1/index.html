The $500k Deal Mechanics

The standard deal is elegant, non-negotiable, and heavily favors the accelerator in downside scenarios while participating in the upside. It bifurcates into two instruments:

  • The Fixed Stake

    $125,000 for a guaranteed 7% ownership on a post-money SAFE.

  • The Variable Stake (MFN)

    $375,000 on an Uncapped SAFE with a "Most Favored Nation" provision. It converts at the lowest valuation cap the startup negotiates with subsequent investors.

The 10-Minute Interrogation

No decks. No small talk. Rapid-fire testing across four vectors:

Problem Space

Do founders have deep, often personal familiarity with an obscure but valuable problem?

Market Dynamics

Is the TAM real? Why is now the exact right time to build this? (The "Why Now" vector).

Velocity

Can this team ship? Evidence of rapid iteration, initial traction, or paying users.

Chemistry

Irrational determination and evidence the founders won't break under extreme pressure.
